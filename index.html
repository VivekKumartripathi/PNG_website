About us

PNG & ASSOCIATES is a chartered accountancy firm led by skilled and experienced young professional chartered accountants founded in the year 2013. The firm has strived with the mission to provide a comprehensive range of financial and consulting services to its clients.We are committed to provide consistent and practical solutions to our clients with a personal touch and strive to support our services.

The Firm provides Accounting, Audit, Assurance, Taxation and Consultancy Services. Our objective is to work with businesses as an extended arm primarily for managing in a manner that will increase profit, reduce cost and provide total control over the activity with the use of trained resources, well defined processes and mutually agreed productivity measures.

Mission

Our mission is to remain a pre-eminent chartered accounting firm by providing value added professional services on a cost effective basis by adhering to an uncompromising commitment to professionalism, integrity, creativity, competence, and quality of service.

Vision

To be the most respected, independent and premier auditing,tax and business consulting firm by offering excellent service and support to clients across the globe.

Strengths

In-depth knowledge and understanding of our clients and their businesses Bringing our experience and expertise to our clients in advising on solutions to their issues Business advisory, audit and accounting, secretarial, financial planning and taxation services Extensive client base, coming from all areas of business, evidence of the breadth of professional expertise that we have to offer A quality professional service is provided to all of our clients by skilled and dedicated team members.
